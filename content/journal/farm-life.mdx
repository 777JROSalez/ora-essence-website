## Harvest Season in Vermont

Fall is a busy time on the Ora Essence farm. As the leaves turn gold and red, we are busy harvesting the last of our summer crops.

### Calendula & Arnica

These two powerhouses are at their peak right now. We hand-pick every flower to ensure maximum potency for our regenerative formulas.
